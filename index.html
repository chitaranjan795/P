Let's Take A Trip To The Iconic State Odisha

ODISHA

Odisha province was founded on the 1st April 1936, and the anniversary of this is celebrated as UTKAL DIBAS throughout the state. The earlist name of odisha was Odra, Kalinga, Utkal, Orissa and Odisha. In that way the capital of odisha was transformed from "jajpur" and "Cuttack" to "Bhubaneswar". Especially the odisha is famous for the "Temple" , "Historycal Place" and "Beaches". A lot of tourists arrives odisha cause of the Places. Odisha an eastern Indian state on the Bay of Bengal, is known for its cultures and its many ancient Hindu temples. The capital, Bhubaneswar, is home to hundreds of temples, The Lingaraj Temple complex, dating to the 11th century.

Raja

The biggest festival of odisha is raja and it is specially for girls. The duration of rajais Read More... three days.Unmarried girls will in new wear traditional Saree and Alata (red dye) on their feet. Indoor and outdoor games are a popular pursuit during Raja. Girls will play on swings tied on tree branches while singing folk songs. There are various types of swings, each with a name such as 'Ram Doli','Pata Doli' and 'Dandi Doli'.

konark

The Sun Temple was built in the 13th century and designed as a chariot of the Sun God, Surya, with twelve pairs of ornamented wheels pulled by seven horses. Some of the wheels are 3 metres wide. Only six of the seven horse still stand today. It was built 1250 CE by King Narasimhadeva. The architecure style by "Bishu Maharana".

puri

The Jagannath temple of puri was renowned by Ananta Varman Chodaganga during 12th century However, later the construction got completed by Ananga Bhima Dev.

Chandipur

Chandipur, a beach in Baleswar District. Odisha is the location of the Indian Strategic Forces Command's Integrated Test Range (ITR) at Abdul Kalam Island, formerly known as Wheeler Island. A number of Indian missiles have been tested and launched from the ITR, including nuclear-capable Prithvi, Agni and Shaurya ballistic missiles, as well as Akash and Barak 8 surface-to-air missiles.

Chilika

Chilika Lake is a brackish water lagoon. It is the largest wintering ground for migratory birds on the Indian sub-continent. Chilika is a place in Odisha which is famous for natural beauty,migratory birds,fishes, dolphins,islands,water sports and most important Kalijai temple.

Jirang

Jirang monastery, A beautiful buddhist temple in odisha. Jirang is a place in odisha which is famous for its beautiful Buddhist monastery. It is located in Chandragiri, Gajapati district, Odisha state. It is a residence-cum-college for budding Buddhist monks who come from all over India to study.
